Realtime Rock Paper Scissors Classifier

This is Browser Based Tensorflow.js Classifier, which means the model is trained and tested on the Client Side using the Browser's own resources, so don't worry if your browser slows down a bit while training.

Providing Examples: To provide a rock training example to the model, you should make a rock symbol with your hand and press the rock button, do the same with paper and scissor. You can see the total number of 'examples provided' below.

Training: Once done with the examples, click the Train Network button and you will start seeing the training statistics on the left side of your browser (don't worry if you're not familiar with some of the terms, they're just for nerds)

Predicting: Now click the Start Predicting button and see the predictions change as you make sybmols with your hand
